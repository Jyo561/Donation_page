Similipal Regen Fund



Similipal National Park is home to a variety of flora and fauna.
It is home to

  • 42 species of mammals
  • 242 species of birds
  • 30 species of reptiles




But recently, due to excessive summer heat a forest fire broke out burning 1/3rd's of Similipal's forest cover.This particular event had not only damaged the biosphere, but also has had dangerous consequences on the flora and fauna as well as the tribes of that area.




So it is important that the forest cover is reinstated as soon as possible, which is possible only and only through reforestration.



A token of help, how small it might be ,
will help in reinstating the nature's beauty!
